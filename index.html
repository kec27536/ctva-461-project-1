Why You Should Invest In Vinyl Records

By: Kevin Castellanos

A Vinyl record is also known as a phonograph record. Vinyls are a analog sound storage medium, that takes the shape of a flat disc. Vinyls have spiral grooves inside which allow them to store music. The groove typically starts towards the edge of the disc, and ends near the center. The phonograph disc record was the primary medium used for music reproduction throughout the 20th century.
